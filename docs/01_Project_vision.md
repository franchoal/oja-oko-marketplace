# Oja-Oko Marketplace

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

---

# Project Vision

To build Nigeria's most trusted digital agricultural marketplace that connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminates unnecessary middlemen, promotes fair pricing, improves market access, and strengthens food security through technology.

---

# Mission

To empower farmers with digital tools that enable them to showcase their agricultural products, reach wider markets, increase profitability, and conduct secure transactions while giving buyers access to fresh, affordable, and traceable farm produce.

---

# Problem Statement

Agricultural marketing in many parts of Nigeria is still largely informal.

Farmers face numerous challenges including:

- Limited access to buyers
- Price exploitation by middlemen
- Poor market visibility
- Post-harvest losses
- Low bargaining power
- Limited digital presence

Buyers also experience challenges such as:

- Difficulty locating trusted farmers
- Inconsistent product quality
- Price uncertainty
- Limited information about product origin
- Time-consuming sourcing processes

These challenges reduce profitability for farmers and increase costs for buyers.

---

# Solution

Oja-Oko Marketplace provides a secure digital platform where farmers can list agricultural products and buyers can discover, compare, and purchase directly from verified farmers.

The platform will provide:

- Farmer registration
- Buyer registration
- Product listings
- Product search
- Category browsing
- Secure ordering
- Digital payments
- Delivery coordination
- Notifications
- Order tracking
- Administrative management

---

# Target Users

## Primary Users

### Farmers

Individuals or agricultural businesses who produce farm products and wish to sell directly to buyers.

### Buyers

Individuals, households, retailers, restaurants, food processors, wholesalers, and organizations purchasing agricultural products.

### Administrators

Platform managers responsible for user management, product moderation, dispute resolution, reporting, and platform administration.

# Guiding Principles

Every feature developed for Oja-Oko Marketplace should satisfy at least one of the following principles:

- Improve farmer experience.
- Improve buyer experience.
- Increase marketplace trust.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Support long-term scalability.
- Maintain clean and secure architecture.

Features that do not align with these principles should not be added without revising the product specification.

# Development Philosophy

The platform will be developed using a modular architecture.

Every module must:

- Be independently maintainable.
- Be fully documented.
- Expose secure REST APIs.
- Support web and mobile clients.
- Follow clean coding standards.
- Be scalable without major redesign.
